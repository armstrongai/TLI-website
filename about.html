About

Who We Are

The Learning Index is a multidisciplinary educational services organization providing high-quality support for students, families, and school districts across Texas.

Our Mission

To ensure that every student receives the tools, instruction, and services they need to succeed. We focus on meaningful outcomes, equitable support, and practical solutions that help teams do their best work.

The TLI Difference

We do not simply fill gaps. We provide thoughtful support built on consistency, collaboration, and high-quality service delivery. Our work is designed to strengthen evaluation practice, improve documentation clarity, and help teams navigate complex responsibilities with confidence.

What We Bring to Districts

The Learning Index supports evaluation teams through professional development, district-level consultation, and tools that respect the realities of school-based practice. We understand timelines, compliance expectations, and the professional judgment required in every evaluation.

  • Professional development and training for evaluation teams
  • District evaluation support and consultation
  • FERPA-conscious workflow tools, including Redact AI
  • EVALed™ conference and practitioner-centered learning experiences

Our Commitment

We believe evaluation work should be clear, defensible, and manageable. Our goal is to help schools and professionals strengthen systems, protect student information, and make thoughtful use of modern tools without compromising quality or ethics.
