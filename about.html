About The Learning Index

The Learning Index was created to support schools, districts, and evaluation professionals in navigating the growing demands of special education assessment and compliance. With experience in psychoeducational evaluation, report writing, and educational systems, The Learning Index understands the realities of balancing timelines, student needs, documentation requirements, and professional responsibility.

The mission of The Learning Index is to provide practical, high-quality support that helps evaluation teams work more efficiently. Through professional development, consultation, evaluation support, and innovative workflow solutions, The Learning Index is committed to helping professionals strengthen their systems without sacrificing quality.

Whether supporting individual evaluators or entire district teams, the focus remains the same: creating clearer processes, stronger collaboration, and more confident educational decisions for students.
