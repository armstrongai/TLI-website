FAQ

Is this “FERPA certified”?

No vendor can hand you a magic badge that replaces your professional judgment. Redact AI is built to help you remove sensitive details before you use AI. Your district policies and processes still lead.

What exactly gets redacted?

The goal is identifiers and details that shouldn’t travel into a model, including names, dates of birth, addresses, and other student-specific markers you point the tool at.

Who is it for?

Diagnosticians, related service providers, and teams who write evaluation language and are tired of choosing between speed and caution.
