Services

Supporting Better Evaluations, Stronger Teams, and Confident Decisions

The Learning Index provides evaluation-focused support for schools, districts, and special education professionals through professional development, consultation, assessment services, and workflow solutions designed for today’s educational environments.

Professional Development & Training

Practical training opportunities designed specifically for educational diagnosticians, school psychologists, and special education teams.

Topics may include:

  • SLD identification and eligibility considerations
  • Dyslexia and reading-related disabilities
  • Psychoeducational assessment practices
  • Bilingual and culturally responsive evaluation
  • AI and workflow efficiency for evaluation professionals
  • Report writing and documentation practices
  • Ethics, compliance, and educational decision-making

Training opportunities are designed to provide immediately applicable strategies that support both compliance and confidence in practice.
