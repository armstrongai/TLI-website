Redact AI

Protect Student Information Before Using AI

Redact AI is a workflow tool designed to help educational professionals remove personally identifiable student information before using AI-supported platforms for drafting, organizing, or refining documentation.

As AI becomes more common in education, many professionals are looking for ways to improve efficiency while still protecting sensitive student information. Redact AI was created to support safer, more responsible workflows for special education and evaluation professionals.

Key Features

  • Helps remove identifying student information before using AI tools
  • Supports FERPA-conscious workflow practices
  • Designed for education-based documentation and report writing workflows
  • Helps professionals save time while maintaining professional responsibility
  • Simple and practical workflow integration

Why Redact AI?

Evaluation professionals are balancing increasing documentation demands, tight timelines, and growing interest in AI-supported workflow tools. Redact AI helps bridge the gap between efficiency and responsible practice by encouraging safer handling of sensitive information before using AI platforms.

The Goal

The goal of Redact AI is not to replace professional judgment. It is to support professionals by creating cleaner, safer, and more efficient workflows.
